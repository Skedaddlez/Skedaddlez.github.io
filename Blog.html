08/05/2019

The Prusa cometh...

Over the weekend, my Prusa i3 MK3S arrived! After hours and hours and hours of building followed by more hours of calibration, I finally am getting some decent prints. So far, I am very impressed by the ability of this printer, even despite the price tag. My plan is to use this to make enclosures to my projects, as well as repairing and embellishing other things when it makes sense. One particular print I am looking forward to is the case for my Wii portable I plan to make this year!

23/04/2019

One likely dead Uno later...

In trying to get get a second GC randomiser circuit up and running, I managed to overheat an Arduino Uno clone's ATmega328 chip to a temperature easily high enough to burn my fingers off.

After hours slaving away, turns out I had been looking at the wrong pinout the whole time - I needed the female and I was looking at the male. I was surging the poor Arduino with a negative voltage as a result, as 5V and ground were mirrored from male to female. Whoops.

It also cost me at least an extra GC extension cable, but hey, you learn.
